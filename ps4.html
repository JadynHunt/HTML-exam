• Playstation 4
  • Image from the wikipedia webpage linked in "Playstation 4" name. Photographer unknown. Public domain.

  • The Playstation 4 was released in November in North America, South America, Europe, and Australia. It was released February 22, 2014 in Japan. Its competition was the Xbox 1, Wii U, and the Nintendo Switch. The Playstation 4 introduced new concepts into the gaming world that were unthinkable. The online gaming capabilities were heavily increased bringing gamers from everywhere together. Share play was introduced which allows someone to let a friend play with them on their Playstation from another Playstation. It’s like playing with someone at your house, but they’re able to play from their house. The Playstation 4 also allows 4k resolution which is one of the best resolutions to date. You can play virtual reality (VR) games through your ps4 if you have the VR headset. On September 7, 2016 the Playstation 4 Slim and the Playstation 4 Pro were released. Not only the system but also the broad exclusive games that the Playstation 4 offers, attracts fans. So far, 113.5 million units have been sold worldwide making it the 4th most sold console in history. They are still being produced and sold today.
