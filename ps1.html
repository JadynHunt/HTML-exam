  • The Playstation is the first of Sony’s game console series and was released in 1994 in japan and was not spread to other parts of the world ( North America, Europe, and Australia) until September of 1995. Ken Kutaragi, a Sony executive, is responsible for the idea and creation of the Playstation so he was later called “The Father of Playstation”. The console had competition with the Nintendo 64 and the Sega Saturn. In the year 2000 the Playstation was redesigned and was replaced by the Playstation 1 . The number 1 was added to the title so that the public didn’t confuse it as the second PlayStation (Playstation 2). One of the features of the Playstation 1 that made it so popular, is the console’s use of compact discs (CDs). Most game consoles at this time used cartridges. This console boosted Sony as a company and when they allowed third-party companies to make games for their console it boosted their popularity even more. The Playstation 1 became the very first console to sell and ship 100 million units. The creation of the first Playstation ended on March 23, 2006.
